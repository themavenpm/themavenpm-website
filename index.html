About The Maven PM

Built by a PM Who Has Been Exactly Where You Are

The Maven PM was founded by LaShondra S. Hubert, MBA, PMP, a capital construction and program management professional with more than 12 years of experience across mission critical infrastructure, healthcare, and transit. She has led projects totaling over $650M across organizations including Grady Health System, Gilbane Building Company, MARTA, and Amazon Web Services.

The Maven PM exists because project management is a career with real pathways, and too many talented professionals are left to navigate it alone.
